Cancellation Policy

Trip Cancellation by the Customer

If you need to cancel your trip, please notify us as soon as possible...

Our cancellation policy is as follows:

  • Cancellations made 30 days or more prior to the trip start date will receive a full refund.
  • Cancellations made between 15 to 29 days prior to the trip start date will be eligible for a 50% refund.
  • No refunds will be provided for cancellations made less than 15 days prior to the trip start date.

Please note that any non-refundable fees or expenses incurred by Adventour will be deducted from the refund amount.

Trip Cancellation by Adventour

In the unlikely event that we need to cancel a trip due to unforeseen circumstances...

We will provide the following options to our customers:

  • Full refund of the trip cost.
  • Transfer to an alternative trip of equal value, subject to availability.

We will make every effort to notify customers in advance if a trip needs to be canceled.
