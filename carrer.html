Adventure Guide

We are seeking an experienced adventure guide to join our team...

Responsibilities:

  • Lead groups on thrilling adventure trips, ensuring safety and enjoyment of participants.
  • Provide guidance and support to participants during activities such as hiking, climbing, and water sports.
  • Share knowledge and insights about the destinations and activities to enhance the experience of participants.
  • Ensure adherence to safety protocols and emergency procedures.

Requirements:

  • Minimum of 3 years of experience as an adventure guide.
  • Strong communication and leadership skills.
  • Valid first aid and CPR certification.
  • Experience in wilderness navigation and outdoor survival skills.

If you are passionate about adventure and have the required skills and experience, we would love to hear from you!

Marketing Specialist

We are looking for a creative and results-driven marketing specialist to join our team...

Responsibilities:

  • Develop and implement marketing strategies to promote our adventure trips and attract new customers.
  • Create engaging content for website, social media, and other marketing channels.
  • Manage advertising campaigns and analyze their performance to optimize results.
  • Monitor market trends and competitor activities to identify opportunities and make data-driven decisions.

Requirements:

  • Bachelor's degree in marketing or related field.
  • Proven experience in digital marketing and social media management.
  • Strong analytical skills and proficiency in marketing analytics tools.
  • Excellent written and verbal communication skills.

If you have a passion for adventure and marketing, we would love to have you on our team!
